About Us

Teriyaki Sushi House offers the finest Japanese, Thai, and Chinese cuisine in Centurion. Our skilled chefs prepare fresh sushi daily, using only the highest quality ingredients to ensure an authentic dining experience.

From classic nigiri sushi to special rolls, flavorful Thai curries, and Chinese favorites - we have something for everyone to enjoy!
